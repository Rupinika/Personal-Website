My Journey

My educational journey has been a path of growth, discovery, and transformation. It began at SSCPS (South Shore Charter Public School), where I first discovered my love for learning and community. From there, I moved to Woodland Elementary, where I began to explore my interests more deeply and developed a sense of curiosity about the world around me.

At Stacy Middle School, I truly began to come into my own. It was here that I discovered my passion for technology and leadership. I learned to balance academics with extracurricular activities, and I started to understand the importance of being an active participant in my school community. The challenges I faced and the opportunities I embraced during these formative years helped shape my character and ambitions.

Now at Milford High School, I continue to grow and evolve. I've matured into someone who values hard work, curiosity, and determination. Each school has been a stepping stone, teaching me valuable lessons about resilience, adaptability, and the importance of staying true to myself while pushing boundaries and exploring new possibilities.

My Values

Curiosity

I believe in asking questions, exploring new ideas, and never stopping the pursuit of knowledge. This curiosity drives my passion for computer science and technology.

Leadership

I strive to lead by example, inspire others, and create positive change in my community. Leadership is about empowering others and working collaboratively toward common goals.

Determination

I approach challenges with resilience and persistence. Whether it's mastering a new programming language or running for class president, I commit fully to achieving my goals.

Culture & Heritage

My connection to my culture and religion, Hinduism, is an integral part of who I am. Celebrating festivals like Holi has taught me about the importance of community, joy, and coming together with others. These experiences have shaped my outlook on life, reminding me that there's beauty in diversity, strength in unity, and joy in celebration.

My cultural background has instilled in me values of respect, compassion, and gratitude. It has also given me a unique perspective that I bring to everything I do, whether it's working on a technology project, leading a school club, or collaborating with peers from different backgrounds.

Looking Forward

As I continue my journey, I'm excited about the opportunities ahead. My goal is to study computer science at MIT, where I can further develop my technical skills and contribute to innovations that make a difference. I'm committed to continuing to grow as a leader, an innovator, and a person who makes a positive impact on the world around me.
